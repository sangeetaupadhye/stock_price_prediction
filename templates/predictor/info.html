Model Info & Help

Understanding the AI & how to use it

Model Architecture

Type LSTM Neural Network
Layer 1 LSTM — 50 units, return sequences
Layer 2 LSTM — 50 units
Layer 3 Dense — 1 unit (output)
Input Shape 60 timesteps × 1 feature
Output Next closing price

Training Data

Stock Reliance Industries
Data Range 5 Years Historical
Feature Used Close Price
Scaling MinMax (0 to 1)
Time Step 60 days lookback

How to Use

1
Login

Sign in to your account to access all features.

2
Upload Your Data

Go to Data Cleaning and upload a CSV file containing stock data. Make sure it has a close_price column with at least 60 rows.

3
Generate Forecast

Go to Predict Price, select your forecast range (1 day, 7 days, or 30 days) and click Generate.

4
Analyse the Chart

The result page shows historical prices vs predicted prices on a chart. Blue line = historical, red dashed = predicted.

5
View Live Charts

Go to View Charts and enter any NSE stock symbol (e.g. TCS.NS, INFY.NS) to see live 6-month price data with moving average.

Important Notes

1-day forecast High accuracy
7-day forecast Moderate accuracy
30-day forecast Lower accuracy
CSV format Must have close_price column
Min rows needed At least 60 rows
